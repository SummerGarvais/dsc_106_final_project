01  /  Sea Ice Thickness

Initial Prototype Writeup

So far we have implemented a rough design of the website with our main mechanism of interaction, the ability to easily change the year and month, being implemented and functional. We haven’t implemented specific texts relating to our ideas, but through titles and placeholder texts we have been able to sketch a rough flow of ideas for a user reading our visualization. A feature to keep the slider for interaction in a constant place at the top of the page during scrolling makes interacting consistent and continuous. We have further used our section titles as ways to sketch out what potential interactions and visualizations could happen.

Given our current prototype we find the challenge being added more nuanced interactions and examples to support our narrative. With the general time slider we can already provide lots of unique, yet less specific, information. Searching for specific examples in regions or time periods that correlate to certain policy or technology changes would be one task that could lead us to this. So designating time for finding specific examples within the dataset, world, and potentially other project 3 data sets to further explore ideas serves as a major step going forward.
